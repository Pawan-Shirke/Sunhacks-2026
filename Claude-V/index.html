Latest Regulatory Updates

Real-time tracking of RBI, SEBI, and MCA circulars with AI-powered risk assessment

🏦 RBI 🔴 HIGH RISK
Master Direction — Know Your Customer (KYC) Amendment 2024
📅 15 November 2024 · Banking / KYC
RBI mandates re-verification of pre-2020 accounts via Aadhaar eKYC, beneficial ownership declarations for corporate accounts above ₹10L, and Video KYC support. Non-compliance attracts up to ₹1 crore penalty under PMLA.
+ eKYC re-verification mandatory for pre-2020 accounts
+ Beneficial ownership declaration for corporate accounts > ₹10L
- Removed: Self-declaration waiver for KYC-compliant customers
+ New: 90-day compliance deadline enforced
📈 SEBI 🔴 HIGH RISK
Enhanced Disclosure Requirements for IPO Filings — ICDR Amendment
📅 28 October 2024 · Capital Markets / IPO
SEBI extends related-party disclosure window from 3 to 5 years. ESG/BRSR reporting mandatory for turnover above ₹500 crore. Merchant bankers must certify DRHP completeness under personal liability.
+ RPT disclosure window: 3 years → 5 years
+ ESG/BRSR mandatory for turnover > ₹500 Cr
+ Merchant banker personal liability certification
- Removed: Simplified disclosure for SME-IPOs < ₹50 Cr
🏛️ MCA 🟡 MEDIUM RISK
Companies (Amendment) Rules 2024 — Annual Filing Compliance
📅 10 September 2024 · Corporate Compliance
MCA reduces AOC-4 filing deadline to 45 days for listed companies, mandates XBRL for all public companies regardless of turnover, and introduces new e-CSR-1 form for CSR spends above ₹50 lakh.
+ AOC-4 filing: 60 days → 45 days for listed companies
+ XBRL mandatory for ALL public companies (no turnover threshold)
+ New e-CSR-1 form for CSR spends > ₹50L
- Removed: Physical filing option for certain forms
📈 SEBI 🔴 HIGH RISK
Cybersecurity & Cyber Resilience Framework (CSCRF) for Market Infrastructure
📅 5 July 2024 · Cybersecurity / Technology
SEBI mandates CISO appointment, cyber incident reporting within 6 hours (down from 24h), quarterly VAPT by CERT-In auditor, and 3-year technology roadmap submission by December 2024 for all MIIs and large brokers.
+ CISO appointment mandatory, reports to Board
+ Cyber incident reporting: 24h → 6 hours
+ Quarterly VAPT by CERT-In empanelled auditor
- Removed: Annual self-certification option
🏦 RBI 🔴 HIGH RISK
Prudential Framework for Resolution of Stressed Assets — Updated
📅 22 August 2024 · Banking / NPA
ICA signing deadline extended to 45 days post-SMA-2 classification. Provisioning requirement increased to 35% (from 20%) for accounts in resolution beyond 180 days. Real-time CRILC integration mandatory by March 2025.
+ ICA deadline: 30 days → 45 days post-SMA-2
+ Provision requirement: 20% → 35% (beyond 180 days)
+ Real-time CRILC integration by March 2025
- Removed: 60-day grace period for restructuring
